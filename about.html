About Us

WDH Newsroom provides the latest news, headlines, and events from Wentworth-Douglass Hospital. The site’s goal is to provide patients, staff, and journalists with up-to-date information regarding current events at Wentworth-Douglass. The page is published and maintained by the hospital’s Community Relations Department.

Meet the Team

Adam Bagni

Director, Communications & Community

Adam Bagni is responsible for developing, and executing, Wentworth-Douglass’ comprehensive internal and external communication, community engagement, and public relations strategies. He also serves as the hospital’s primary spokesman and manages its guest relations team.

A former award-winning television journalist, Bagni had a long career in broadcast journalism, earning him numerous regional and statewide honors, including 10 EMMY nominations. He most recently worked as a reporter at WCVB-5 in Boston. Bagni also had stints as an anchor/reporter at KPNX-12 in Phoenix, WHDH-7 in Boston, WJAR-10 in Providence/New Bedford, and WNCF-32 in Montgomery, AL.

mediarequest@wdhospital.org

Crystal Mockler

Communication Specialist

Crystal Mockler assists in developing and coordinating the hospital’s strategic internal and external communications efforts. Crystal also fulfills a wide variety of public relations, media relations, and social media needs.

Prior to joining Wentworth-Douglass, Crystal spent six years as the Manager of Community Relations for Northeast Delta Dental in Concord. She also worked for three years in Media Relations at Dana-Farber Cancer Institute in Boston. She has a Master of Science in Health Communication from Tufts University School of Medicine.

mediarequest@wdhospital.org

Doug Moon

Video Producer

Doug Moon fulfills a wide variety of video and photography production needs to support the hospital’s communications, media relations, and social media. His assists in conceptualizing, creating, and publishing videos and photographs that amplify strategic internal and external communications efforts.

Prior to joining Wentworth-Douglass, Doug spent more than 20 years in journalism, including as a photographer at WHDH-7 in Boston, WMAQ-5 in Chicago, and WISH-8 in Indianapolis. He is a winner of the prestigious Peabody Award (for work that changed policy at The Pentagon regarding traumatic brain injury for the armed forces) and multiple EMMY Awards for his contributions as a producer, photographer, and editor.

mediarequest@wdhospital.org

Aaron Sanborn

Video Producer

Aaron Sanborn assists in developing and coordinating the hospital’s strategic internal and external communications efforts. Sanborn also fulfills a wide variety of public relations, media relations, and social media needs.

Prior to joining Wentworth-Douglass, Aaron spent more than 13 years at the Seacoast Media Group working as a reporter and editor for Foster’s Daily Democrat, The Portsmouth Herald, and the Exeter News-Letter.

During his time with Seacoast Media, Aaron won multiple awards from the New Hampshire and New England Press Associations for breaking news and feature stories, including for his coverage of the 2007 hostage situation at the Hillary Clinton campaign office in Rochester and the 2012 hepatitis C outbreak at Exeter Hospital.

Most recently, Aaron worked as a content writer for career coaching website Work It Daily.
